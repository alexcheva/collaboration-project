Meia's Fav Tea: Chocolate Strawberry

I found this tea from the Dollar Tea Club.

This herbal tea is shipped from Canada. I like it because it's like a hot cup of dessert. From the point that you add the tea to a cup, to right before you drink it, you'll experience a strong fragrance of chocolate covered strawberries.

The Ingredients:

  • apple
  • hibiscus
  • rosehip
  • strawberry
  • papaya
  • cocoa pieces
  • cornflower petals
  • natural flavors (organic compliant)

    • Honey Lavender

    Personally I'm a fan of milk teas, but when having regular teas, but when I'm drinking regular tea, honey lavender is a personal comfort favorite.
    I'm not a fan of lavender , but this combination is perfect: a little sweet, a little refreshing, and very calming overall.
